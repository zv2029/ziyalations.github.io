About Me

Hi, I'm Ziya. I am an NYU Abu Dhabi student, double majoring in Literature and in Interactive Media. Self-proclaimed wordsmith. I have numerous interests and passions, one of them being the constant pursuit of interlinking my love for the words on the pages and coding creatively. I have a creative vision of exploring new ways to tell stories through the digital domain. My current focus is to experiment with new forms of narrative expression through code. Aside from that, I also am considering a career in the publishing world, or journalism. Storytelling is central to my identity, and I hope that wherever I end up, I can continue this.
